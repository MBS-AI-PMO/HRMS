Employee Flow Guide

Day-to-day guide for staff using the Employee Portal

After login, employees land on the Employee Dashboard at /employee/dashboard. This document covers only the employee journey — not admin, HR, or client portal screens.

What is an employee in HRMS?

An employee is a staff member with role 3 and an employee record linked to their user account. They use the Employee Portal for self-service: attendance, leave, profile, payslips, announcements, and assigned projects/tasks. They see only their own data unless they are also assigned as a team leader, location head, or department head (a small extra slice is described at the end).

Login flow

Employee opens login page → enters username & password → system validates credentials → Employee Dashboard (/employee/dashboard)

Login checks username and password only. If clock-in is enabled, some companies also require you to be on office internet (IP-based) or within GPS range (location-based) when marking attendance.

How employees enter the system

HR/Admin adds employee — HR creates your user account and employee record. Login credentials may be emailed to you.

Public registration — If enabled, you fill the registration form yourself. The system creates an active user + employee (role 3) and emails your login details. Fields and defaults (shift, department, etc.) are set by your company under Settings → Employee Registration.

Employee Dashboard — overview

After login you see your name, designation, department, last login, and today’s shift times. The dashboard is your home screen for daily work.

  • Profile — open your full employee profile
  • Clock IN / Clock OUT — mark attendance (when enabled by your company)
  • Payslip — quick link to your salary slips
  • Awards — view awards received
  • Announcements — company notices
  • Upcoming Holidays — planned holidays
  • Leave / WFH / Travel / Complain — view history or submit new requests
  • Assigned Projects & Tasks — work assigned to you by HR or project managers

Attendance — clock in & clock out

Employee opens Dashboard → clicks Clock IN at shift start → works through the day → clicks Clock OUT at end of shift → attendance is recorded for that date

Your shift times (start/end) come from the office shift assigned to you. If your company enables overtime, you may see Overtime Clock IN/OUT on off-days or after regular shift hours.

Attendance types (set by HR on your employee record):

  • General — clock in/out from the dashboard without extra checks
  • IP-based — you must be on the office network to clock in/out; button is disabled otherwise
  • Location-based — browser may ask for GPS; you must be within the allowed radius of your assigned location

You cannot edit company-wide attendance records. View your own attendance history from Profile → Attendance (if shown on your profile).

Leave flow

Dashboard → Leave → Request (or Profile → Leave tab) → select leave type & dates → submit → status = Pending → approver acts → you receive notification → status becomes Approved or Rejected

From the dashboard you can also click View Info to see leave balance and past requests on your profile.

Who approves? Usually your team leader, department head, location head, or HR — whichever applies to your organization. You do not approve your own leave.

Leave types (CL, SL, ML, etc.) and balances are configured by HR. You can only apply for types assigned to you.

Work From Home (WFH) flow

Dashboard → WFH → Request (or Profile → WFH tab) → select dates & reason → submit → pending approval → notification on decision

WFH works like leave but uses WFH-specific leave types. Track status from the same profile section or dashboard cards.

Travel flow

Dashboard → Travel → Request (or Profile → Travel tab) → fill travel details (dates, purpose, mode) → submit → HR/manager reviews → status updated

View past and pending travel requests from your profile. Approved travel may appear on calendars if your company uses the HR calendar module.

Complaints flow

Dashboard → Complain → Open (or Profile → Complaints tab) → describe the issue → submit ticket → HR/support reviews → status updated

Use this to raise workplace complaints or support tickets. You see only your own complaints, not other employees’.

Profile

Open Profile from the dashboard hero area or sidebar. Your profile is the central place for personal and job information.

  • Basic info — name, contact, designation, department, joining date
  • Change password — update your login password
  • Documents, qualifications, work experience, bank account (as enabled)
  • Leave, WFH, Travel, Complaints — history and new requests
  • Payslip — download or view monthly salary slips
  • Awards — recognition received
  • Activity logs — your recent actions in the system (if enabled)

You can update fields that HR allows employees to edit (e.g. contact details, photo). Sensitive HR fields may be read-only.

Payslip

Dashboard → Payslip card → Profile → Payslip section → select month → view or download PDF

You see only your own payslips. Payroll is generated by HR; employees do not run payroll or pay other staff.

Announcements & holidays

Announcements — Click the announcement card on the dashboard or open Announcements from the sidebar (if visible). Read company-wide notices posted by HR.

Holidays — The dashboard shows upcoming holidays count. Click to see dates and titles. Holidays follow your company’s holiday calendar and assigned office shift.

Projects

HR assigns you to a project → project appears on Dashboard under Assigned Projects → click to open details

Employees view and work on assigned items only. They do not create company projects or assign other employees unless given separate HR permissions.

Notifications

When leave/WFH is approved or rejected, or when HR sends notices, you may get in-app notifications (bell icon) and email. Open notifications from the top bar to see recent updates.

Password change

Profile → Change Password → enter current password & new password → save → use new password on next login

Data you can see (simple rule)

Regular employee — own attendance, leave, WFH, travel, complaints, payslips, profile, announcements, holidays, and assigned projects/tasks.

Not visible — other employees’ records, company payroll runs, HR master data, admin settings, or other companies’ data.

Optional: team / location / department leader extras

Most employees use only the flows above. If HR assigns you as a team leader, location head, or department head, extra menus may appear:

  • My Team — view team you belong to or lead
  • Employee List — limited list of your team or location members (not full company)
  • L/ WFH Requests — approve or reject requests from your team, department, or location only
  • My Centers / Locations — for location heads managing their site

These are add-ons on top of the normal employee role. You remain an employee first; leader tools do not turn you into full HR unless admin grants those permissions separately.

Quick mental model

Log in → land on Employee Dashboard → clock in/out for the day → use dashboard cards for leave, WFH, travel, or complaints → check payslip and announcements in Profile → complete assigned projects and tasks. Everything is self-service for your own work life; managers and HR handle approvals and company-wide setup behind the scenes.
